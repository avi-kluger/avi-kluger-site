Feedforward protocols · 2026

Ready-to-run interview guides

The Feedforward Interview replaces feedback with a structured conversation about what already works. These 2026 slide decks walk you through each protocol, step by step.

  • 1 · The Basic ProtocolGuides an interviewer through the five-stage Feedforward Interview, eliciting a peak positive work story and the conditions that enabled it.
  • 2 · Feedforward for a ProblemAdapts the Feedforward Interview to a specific pain point by reframing it into its ideal opposite and interviewing around that.
  • 3 · Feedforward from a HobbyA playful variant that personifies a hobby to surface the interviewee’s values and carry their best qualities into work.
  • 4 · Feedforward for the Shared Team Success CodeRuns the five-stage interview with each team member, then merges their success conditions into one shared Team Success Code.
